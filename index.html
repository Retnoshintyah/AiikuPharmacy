ABOUT US

Vision

To be a pharmaceutical manufacturer that is committed to quality, product safety, and trustworthiness in order to provide significant added value to customers, business partners, and stakeholders through effective, efficient, and sustainable operations.

Mission

  • Meet the requirements and standards of Good Manufacturing Practices.
  • Focusing on innovation and continuous improvement.
  • Providing the best service to consumers and customers so that the company's existence is felt by stakeholders.
  • Participate in efforts to improve the degree of public health in order to realize a better quality of life.

Company Structure

AIIKU is led by a managing director who oversees two directors. Each of these directors oversees managers in each of their areas. Each section manager has the responsibility to ensure that all aspects of GMP are always qualified or determine actions in case of deviation or improvement. Each manager is assisted by an assistant manager in holding regular meetings with employees to always implement GMP in every drug production activity.
